# Branding

## Name

The spelling of **Markuplint** must be all characters are lowercase, or **M** is uppercase, then after are lowercase. And **L** must not be uppercase. And it must not separate **Markup** and **Lint**. It uses the former in command and more. Recommend and usually the latter.

:::note
The initial was lowercase until `v2.x` but changed to uppercase since `v3.0`.
:::

### Abbreviation

The abbreviated name is **ML**. Use `ml-` as a prefix.
